1. Introduction

Welcome to Still Alive ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the App.

Important: Still Alive is designed to provide peace of mind by allowing you to check in regularly and notifying your emergency contacts if you don't. We take the security and privacy of your information seriously and only collect data necessary to provide this service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you register and use the App, we collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Email address and full name
  • Emergency Contacts: Names and email addresses of your designated emergency contacts
  • Check-in Settings: Your preferred check-in interval (number of days)
  • Check-in Data: Timestamps of when you confirm you are alive

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use our App, we may automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Mobile device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App interactions, features used, and timestamps of activities
  • Notification Data: Information about notifications sent and received

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Location or GPS data
  • Contact lists from your device
  • Photos, files, or media from your device
  • Phone numbers
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health or medical information
  • Biometric data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide the Service: To enable you to check in and manage your emergency contacts
  • Send Notifications: To send you reminders to check in via push notifications
  • Emergency Alerts: To send email notifications to your emergency contacts if you miss your check-in deadline
  • Account Management: To maintain and manage your user account
  • Service Improvement: To analyze app usage and improve functionality
  • Security: To protect against fraudulent or illegal activity
  • Communication: To send you important updates about the service (e.g., policy changes)
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and respond to legal requests

4. How We Share Your Information

4.1 Emergency Contacts

When you fail to check in within your configured time interval, we will send email notifications to your designated emergency contacts. This email will include your name, email address, and the date of your last check-in.

4.2 Service Providers

We share your information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services (data hosting and user management)
  • Resend: Email delivery service (sending notifications to emergency contacts)
  • Expo: Mobile app development and push notification services

These service providers are contractually obligated to use your information only for the purposes of providing services to us and are required to maintain appropriate security measures.

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., court orders, subpoenas).

4.4 Business Transfers

If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will provide notice before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different Privacy Policy.

4.5 What We Do NOT Do

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information to third parties
  • Share your information for marketing purposes
  • Share your information with advertisers
  • Use your information for purposes unrelated to the App's core functionality

5. Data Storage and Security

5.1 Data Storage

Your information is stored securely on Supabase servers, which use industry-standard security measures. Data is stored in secure data centers with appropriate physical and electronic safeguards.

5.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of data in transit using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Encryption of sensitive data at rest
  • Secure authentication using industry-standard protocols
  • Row-level security policies in our database
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls limiting who can access your information

5.3 Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or legitimate business purposes.
