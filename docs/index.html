About Still Alive

Still Alive is a mobile application designed to provide peace of mind for you and your loved ones. The app allows users to confirm they are alive by checking in regularly. If a user doesn't check in within their configured time interval, the app automatically sends notifications to their designated emergency contacts.

Key Features

  • Regular check-in reminders
  • Customizable check-in intervals
  • Emergency contact management
  • Automatic email notifications
  • Multi-language support
  • Secure data storage
